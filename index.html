1. About PoputkaGo

PoputkaGo is a digital platform that allows users to publish and view ride-sharing and transportation-related listings.

PoputkaGo does not provide transportation services and is not a taxi or ride-hailing service.

2. Platform Function

The app serves as an informational platform only:

  • users independently publish listings;
  • all trip details are agreed directly between users;
  • PoputkaGo does not set prices or process trip payments.

3. User Responsibility

All users are solely responsible for:

  • compliance with local laws;
  • accuracy of shared information;
  • decisions and agreements made with other users.

PoputkaGo does not verify licenses or qualifications of users.

4. Payments

PoputkaGo does not charge commissions or percentages from trips.

Optional paid features may be introduced for enhanced platform functionality. These services are not transportation services.

5. Future Features

The app may evolve and introduce advanced features or updated versions while maintaining its core platform role.

6. Data Protection

We collect only essential data required for app functionality. User data is handled securely and used solely to support the service.

7. Agreement

By using PoputkaGo, users agree to these terms.
